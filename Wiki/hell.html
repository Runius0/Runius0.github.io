Hell

Hell is a secret sanctuary available by taking a hidden route in the southern room of the base sanctum.

Central room

despite appearing to not have any exit paths, the central room of hell is actually a four way intersection, however only the bottom path is currently implemented.

There is a save point in the center to allow you to save your game.

Exiting out of the bottom will return you to the base sanctum.
